Repetition

will

1. I will go to the cinema tonight.
Irei ao cinema esta noite.

2. Will you tell him the truth?
Você vai contar a verdade para ele?

3. Don't worry, He won't tell anyone.
Não se preocupe, Ele não vai contar a ninguém.

4. She will be happy with her test results.
Ela ficará feliz com os resultados do teste.

5. They will not stay here.
Eles não vão ficar aqui.

6. She answered, "I will pay ten cents only".
Ela respondeu: "Eu vou pagar apenas dez centavos".

7. I will play with you.
Eu vou jogar com você.

going to

8. I'm going to have another cup of coffee.
Vou tomar outra xícara de café.

9. Where are you going to summer?
Onde você vai no verão?

10. He's going to tell his boss he's resigning today.
Ele vai dizer ao seu chefe que está se demitindo hoje.

11. We're going to a match on Sunday.
Vamos a um jogo no domingo.

12. Are they going to sleep all day?
Eles vão dormir o dia todo?

13. He's going to say he loves me.
Ele vai dizer que me ama.

be + going to

14. I am travelling to Japan next week.
Estou viajando para o Japão na próxima semana.

15. She´s travelling to Bahia next month.
Ela está viajando para a Bahia no mês que vem.

16. He´s not getting a new bike.
Ele não vai comprar uma moto nova.

17. We are having a party next month.
Nós vamos ter uma festa no próximo mês.

18. They are visiting us tomorrow.
Eles vão nos visitar amanhã.

19. I am meeting a friend later for lunch.
Vou encontrar um amigo mais tarde para o almoço.

20. She´s not going on vacation.
Ela não vai de férias.

Questions

1. What will you do this weekend?

2. What will life be like when you are 40?

3. Will computers ever take over the world?

4. When will humans live on another planet?

5. What will you do after you finish university?

6. What will you do before you get married?

7. Will humans ever meet aliens? What will the meeting be like?

8. Where will we get our energy when we run out of oil?

9. What will happen if we keep polluting the environment?

10. Will the climate keep changing or go back to normal?

11. Is he going to play basketball on Monday?

12. Are the girls going to ride their bikes at the weekend?

13. Is Jane going to clean the house today?

14. Is his father going to buy a new coat on Saturday?

15. Are they going to meet their friends at the club?

16. Is she going to feed her cat in the evening?

17. Are you going to do your homework tonight?

18. Are Bob and Jane going to work late tomorrow?

19. Are we going to help our father today?

20. Is Jane going to talk to her mother about that topic?
