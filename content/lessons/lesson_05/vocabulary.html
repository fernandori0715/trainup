Click on the colored words to listen.
  1. Get at - (to mean) - querer dizer
    I don´t understand her. What is she getting at?
    Eu não entendo ela. Aonde ela quer chegar?
  2. Be along - (arrive) - chegar
    I hope Jane will be along soon.
    Espero que Jane chegue logo.
  3. Broke into - (to suddenly begin to do something;to open ) - começar, abrir
    He felt so happy that he broke into song
    Ele ficou tão feliz que começou a cantar.
  4. Feeling down - (depressed) - sentir depressivo, para baixo
    I got some bad news from mom this morning. I am feeling down now.
    Recebi más notícias da mãe esta manhã. Estou me sentindo mal agora.
  5. Bring about - (achieve) - considerar
    It helps bring about our most ambitious dreams into reality.
    Ajuda a transformar nossos sonhos mais ambiciosos em realidade.
  6. Come back - (to return to place) - retornar
    We came back stronger than ever as a company.
    Voltamos mais fortes do que nunca como empresa.
  7. Carry off - (to succeed in doing or achieving something difficult) - conseguir algo difícil
    She was nervous about the examn, but she carried it off very well.
    Ela estava nervosa com o exame, mas o conduziu muito bem.
  8. Let you down - (disappoint - criticar
    I promised not to let him down.
    Eu prometi não decepcioná-lo.
  9. Come down with - (to start to suffer from an illness) - ficar doente
    I think I'm coming down with flu.
    Acho que estou pegando uma gripe.
  10. Call off - (cancel) - cancelar
    I have to call off the party.
    Eu tenho que cancelar a festa.
