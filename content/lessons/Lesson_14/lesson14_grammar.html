Unit 113 although / though / even though / in spite of / despite

Although, though, even though

1. I didn’t apply for the job although I had the necessary qualifications.

2. I got up early this morning although it’s the weekend.

3. Although I had a street map, I got lost.

4. John didn’t pass the test although he studied hard.

5. Although it was raining, we went to the beach.

6. Although the prices are a little higher at that supermarket, we like to go shopping there anyway.

7. My house was not empty though I was gone.

8. He was handsome, though she wouldn't go so far as to call him gorgeous.

9. Though he tried hard, nothing changed.

10. The phone woke me up, even though it wasn't very loud.

11. Though my father was very tired, he completed the work.

12. Though He is rich, he leads simple lives.
